Projects

Spectrally-Assisted Sampling for Randomized Kaczmarz

Aiming to address inefficiencies in the standard random sampling distribution for Randomized Kaczmarz and other related algorithms, we explore alternative approaches to promote faster, more reliable convergence on an ill-conditioned subset of overdetermined systems of linear inequalities.

On the Effect of HIV on Tuberculosis Treatment Outcomes in Botswana, Adjusted for Universal Access to Antiretroviral Therapy (ART)

An epidemiological study on the impact of public health policy in Gaborone, Botswana in response to the high prevalence of HIV and its effect on Tuberculosis treatment outcomes.

Hippocampal Replay of Nonspatial Sequences of Events

An exploratory analysis on the neuronal decoding process of odor presentations in the hippocampus; a look into the brain and how understanding it could teach us about treating and/or identifying cognitive diseases related to working memory.
